Welcome to the Yellowstone Nationa Park Database

On March 1, 1872, Yellowstone became the first national park. Every year, many visitors come to explore 2.2 million acres of the beautiful land and its wildlife. We have park rangers located in every region if you need a map or have any other questions. When you visit please keep your distance from the geysers and do not disrupt any of the wildlife, which includes touching and feeding animals. Thank you.
